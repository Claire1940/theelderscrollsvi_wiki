<Section icon={Zap} title="Technical Evolution with Creation Engine 3.0">

At the heart of **the elder scrolls vi iliac** is the debut of Creation Engine 3.0. While Starfield utilized the 2.0 iteration, the 3.0 build is designed specifically to handle the dense, handcrafted environments of a high-fantasy RPG. This technical evolution focuses on "immersion through persistence," where objects and NPC actions have a more permanent impact on the world state.

The most significant change is the removal of compartmentalized loading. In previous games, entering a small shop required a loading transition. In the Iliac build, players can walk directly from a desert dune into a bustling tavern without a single frame of interruption. This fluidity extends to the combat system, which now utilizes high-impact hit-drag mechanics.

| Technical Aspect | CE 2.0 (Starfield) | CE 3.0 (Iliac) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Interior Cells** | Often required loading | Seamless integration |
| **Combat Physics** | Standard hit reactions | Hit-drag and dismemberment |
| **Asset Density** | High (Procedural focus) | Ultra-high (Handcrafted focus) |
| **AI Schedule** | Basic routines | Complex, reactive social AI |
