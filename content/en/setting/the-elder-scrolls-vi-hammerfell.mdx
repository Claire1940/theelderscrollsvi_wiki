<Section icon={BookOpen} title="Historical Eras and the Warrior Wave">

The transition from a Dwemer stronghold to a Redguard kingdom is a central pillar of the lore that will likely drive the main quest in **the elder scrolls vi hammerfell**.

<Callout type="warning" title="Lore Warning">
Hammerfell is currently an independent nation, having seceded from the Empire after the Great War. This creates a unique political vacuum where neither the Thalmor nor the Mede Dynasty has official control.
</Callout>

The Redguards, or the Yokudans, fled their sinking continent of Yokuda in the first era. They were not just refugees but a disciplined military force known as the Ra Gada. This "Warrior Wave" quickly subdued the local beastfolk and Nedic settlements, establishing a culture based on martial honor and the "Way of the Sword."
