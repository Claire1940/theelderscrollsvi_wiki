<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Technology: Creation Engine 3 Features">

The Elder Scrolls VI is being built on the next iteration of Bethesda’s proprietary technology: Creation Engine 3. This engine builds upon the lighting and physics improvements seen in Starfield but focuses more heavily on the needs of a terrestrial fantasy world rather than space exploration.

| Tech Area | Improvement in CE3 | Gameplay Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **World Streaming** | Zero-latency cell loading | Seamless entry into large cities |
| **AI Systems** | Radiant AI 2.0 | NPCs with complex schedules and jobs |
| **Animation** | Physics-based locomotion | More responsive melee and spellcasting |
| **Procedural Tools** | Dense vegetation/ruin placement | Handcrafted feel for a massive map |
| **Physics** | Destructible environments | Spells and weapons affect the world |

<Callout type="tip" title="Modding Support">
Creation Engine 3 is being designed with the "Mod-First" philosophy. Bethesda has confirmed that the tools provided to the community will be the same ones used by the developers to build the world of Tamriel, ensuring a decade of community-driven content.
