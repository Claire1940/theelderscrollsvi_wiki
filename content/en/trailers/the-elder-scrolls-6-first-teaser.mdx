<Section icon={Shield} title="Expected Platforms and Creation Engine 3">

The technical foundation of the game is being built on Creation Engine 3, an evolution of the technology powering Starfield. This engine upgrade focuses on seamless world streaming, advanced physics, and highly detailed character animations to bring western Tamriel to life.

Due to Bethesda's acquisition by Microsoft, the platform landscape has shifted significantly since the original teaser was shown.
