<Section icon={Shield} title="Studio Capacity and Team Management">

A common misconception is that Bethesda Game Studios is a massive conglomerate. In reality, during the development of *Fallout 4*, the team consisted of only around 108 employees. While they have expanded with offices in Montreal and Austin, they still operate with a "small studio" mindset compared to industry giants. This smaller footprint means that when the entire studio shifts focus to a game like *Starfield*, work on other titles effectively slows to a crawl.
