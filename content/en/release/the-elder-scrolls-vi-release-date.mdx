<Section icon={Cpu} title="Technical Prowess: Creation Engine 3.0">

The transition to Creation Engine 3.0 is the backbone of these ambitious features. By overhauling the engine used for Starfield, Bethesda has reportedly solved the "loading screen problem." This engine allows for high-detail assets and complex AI routines to run simultaneously across massive distances.

**Engine Improvements:**
- **Seamless Transitions**: Enter any building or city without a loading bar.
- **Atmospheric Physics**: Real-time weather patterns affecting ship handling and magic.
- **Advanced AI**: NPCs have more complex daily routines and reactive behaviors to player actions.
- **Graphic Fidelity**: Utilizing the latest in photogrammetry to bring the deserts of Hammerfell to life with photorealistic textures.

| Spec Category | Creation Engine 2.0 (Starfield) | Creation Engine 3.0 (TES VI) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Interior Loading** | Necessary for most buildings | Almost entirely eliminated |
| **NPC Density** | Moderate | High (Crowded Cities) |
| **Physics** | Standard | High-Impact Melee/Naval |
| **World Persistence** | High | Extreme (Long-term changes) |
