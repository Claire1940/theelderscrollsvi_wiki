<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Evolution of Bethesda's RPG Engine">

The technical foundation of Bethesda's games has always been a double-edged sword. The Creation Engine allows for unparalleled object persistence and modding support, but it has historically struggled with performance, animations, and loading screens. Understanding this technical evolution explains why development takes so long.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Creation Engine Era">

  <Step num="2" title="The Multiplayer Pivot">
