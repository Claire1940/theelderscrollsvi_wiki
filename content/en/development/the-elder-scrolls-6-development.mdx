Former Xbox CEO Phil Spencer committed to bringing the game exclusively to platforms where Xbox Game Pass is supported, confirming that the title will not launch on competing consoles.

The timeline has also been heavily influenced by sibling projects within the Xbox Game Studios portfolio. Spencer confirmed that the game is scheduled to launch after Playground Games' highly anticipated *Fable* reboot, which is currently slated for a 2027 release. This positioning, combined with leaks indicating Microsoft's plans for next-generation hardware around 2028, makes a **2028 launch window** highly probable.
