Skyrim has maintained a massive, active player base for over fifteen years, largely thanks to its robust modding community and timeless design. To replicate this success, Bethesda must build a foundation that can support decades of play. This begins with significant upgrades to their proprietary technology, the Creation Engine 2.

In terms of design philosophy, the studio is focusing on depth over sheer scale. Howard has expressed a cautious approach to procedural generation, noting that while it is useful for creating broad landscapes, the heart of an Elder Scrolls game lies in discoverable, hand-placed details.
