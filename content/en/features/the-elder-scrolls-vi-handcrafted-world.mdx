<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Technology: Creation Engine 3">

To bring the **the elder scrolls vi handcrafted world** to life, Bethesda is leveraging **Creation Engine 3**. This technology builds upon the foundations of the engine used for *Starfield* but introduces significant upgrades in world streaming, AI routines, and physical interactions. The goal is to eliminate the "loading screen" barrier that has historically separated cities and interiors from the open world.
